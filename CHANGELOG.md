## [0.1.0] - 2023-01-29

### Added

- Two-way conversion between latin and cyrillic script
- Support for bald latin (converting `dz` to `џ` and `dj` to `ђ`)
- Support for converting ijekavian words
- Support for extending dictionary of literal words
